Amazing Great Technology

Ubu ni urugendo rwawe rwo kumenya ikoranabuhanga

1️⃣ Amateka y'Ikoranabuhanga

Reba uko ikoranabuhanga ryagiye ritera imbere mu mateka.

Ikoranabuhanga ryatangiye nko mu gihe cya mbere cy'ibikoresho byoroshye, hanyuma rikagera ku mashini ya mudasobwa n'ikoranabuhanga rigezweho.

2️⃣ Amasomo y'Ikoranabuhanga

Menya ibice by'ingenzi by'ikoranabuhanga n'uburyo bikora.

Amasomo y'ikoranabuhanga arimo: Programming, Networks, Mobile apps, AI, Security, n'ibindi byinshi.

3️⃣ Ibikoresho by'Ikoranabuhanga

Reba ibikoresho by'ingenzi mu ikoranabuhanga rya none.

Ibikoresho birimo computers, smartphones, servers, sensors, na robots, byose bifasha mu iterambere ry’ikoranabuhanga.
